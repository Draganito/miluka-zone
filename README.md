# miluka zone 67 – Medium Format Camera
Revive classic lenses with the miluka zone – simple and small medium format camera body, Graflock 23 back compatible, tested and working with Mamiya RB67 6×7, Mamiya motorized 6×8, and Horseman 6×9 backs.

Salvage classic lenses like Tessar, Novar, Solinar or Apotar from broken folding cameras such as Zeiss Ikonta or Agfa Isolette.

The Miluka Zone is optimized for 6x7 format with lenses between 75 and 105 mm and zone focusing – perfect for fast street photography – while a ground glass enables critical focus and precise composition.

3D-printed or precision-milled from hardwood or even better POM-C – perfect alignment and smooth focusing via M65 helicoids (17–31 mm or 25–55 mm).

If you love the Rollei 35, you’ll absolutely love the miluka zone!
